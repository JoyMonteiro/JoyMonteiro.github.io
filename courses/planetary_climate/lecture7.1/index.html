Longwave vs. Optical depth in a Grey Atmosphere¶

A Grey Atmosphere is an idealisation of a real atmosphere where the gas constituting the atmosphere absorbs uniformly at all frequencies. Or you could think of this atmosphere as being irradiated at a single frequency.

We can see how the OLR changes in a grey atmosphere as we change the optical depth of its constituent gas.
